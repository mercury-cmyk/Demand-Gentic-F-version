Account Cap Enforcement – Contact Selection Priority

When enforcing account caps within verification campaigns, the system must prioritize contacts using the following hierarchy:

Highest Priority – Contacts with both CAV ID and CAV User ID that also match job title and other standard criteria.

Second Priority – Contacts with a complete address and phone number.

Third Priority – Contacts with a phone number plus at least 2–3 postal address fields.

Fourth Priority – Contacts with only a phone number.

No lead should ever be sent to a client without a phone number.

If phone numbers or address fields are missing, the system must automatically trigger AI enrichment to maximize the completion of phone numbers, postal address fields, and other required data.
