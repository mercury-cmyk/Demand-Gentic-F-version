## Outreach Channels & Messaging Themes

### Channel Mix
- **AI Voice Calls** (primary) — 60% of outreach volume. AI agents open conversations, qualify interest, and book meetings.
- **Email Sequences** (support) — 30% of volume. Generative Studio creates persona-specific sequences aligned to org intelligence.
- **Content & Landing Pages** (conversion) — 10% of volume. eBooks, solution briefs, and landing pages as conversion assets.

### Core Messaging Themes

| Theme | Hook | Supporting Proof |
|---|---|---|
| **Consolidation** | "Stop paying for 5 tools that don't talk to each other" | Single platform replaces dialer + email + ABM + intent + analytics |
| **AI-Native, Not AI-Bolted** | "Our AI agents don't just analyze calls — they make them" | Real-time Gemini Live + OpenAI Realtime voice agents |
| **Intelligence-First** | "Every touchpoint is informed by multi-model company research" | Organization Intelligence synthesizes 4+ AI models per prospect |
| **Speed to Pipeline** | "Generate a full campaign — from research to content to calls — in hours, not weeks" | Generative Studio + auto-provisioned AI agents + instant deployment |
| **Quality Over Quantity** | "Verified, qualified leads — not raw lists" | Built-in data integrity checks, QA scoring, and PM review gates |

## Go-To-Market Motion

**Phase 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1–2)**
- Run Organization Intelligence analysis on top 100 target accounts
- Build ICP-aligned messaging using Generative Studio
- Create 3 landing pages (by persona: RevOps, Demand Gen, Sales Dev)
- Develop 3 email sequences (cold outreach, nurture, breakup)

**Phase 2 — Launch (Weeks 3–6)**
- Deploy AI voice agents on Batch 1 (25 accounts)
- Activate email sequences in parallel
- Monitor call quality scores and adjust agent prompts in real time
- A/B test messaging themes across segments

**Phase 3 — Scale (Weeks 7–12)**
- Expand to full 100-account list based on Phase 2 learnings
- Activate AE assignment workflow for qualified pipeline
- Launch eBook + solution brief campaigns for mid-funnel nurture
- Publish campaign analytics dashboard for stakeholder reporting
