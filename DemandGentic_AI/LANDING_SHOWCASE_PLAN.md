# Landing Page Showcase Plan (Implemented Capabilities)

## Objective
Showcase the platform's most differentiated, already-implemented intelligence systems in a way that improves enterprise trust and conversion quality.

## Showcase Principles
- Only present capabilities that exist in production or active implementation paths.
- Pair each capability with a proof signal (what can be demonstrated live).
- Keep narrative order: **Intelligence → Execution → Governance → Outcomes**.
- Make each showcase block answer: "What problem does this solve for RevOps / Demand Gen / Sales?"

## Priority Showcase Systems

### 1) Problem Intelligence + Account Context Layer
**Why it matters:** Moves from generic outreach to account-specific reasoning.

**Show live:**
- Account research context in campaign flow
- Buying committee mapping inputs/outputs
- Trigger-based contextual messaging setup

**Proof signal:**
- Demonstrate one account with role-aware messaging differences

---

### 2) Real-Time AI Voice Calling (WebRTC + Realtime)
**Why it matters:** Conversational outbound with live call actions, not static dialer scripts.

**Show live:**
- Browser-based AI call initiation
- Real-time disposition actions (e.g., callback/disposition workflows)
- Human transfer path where configured

**Proof signal:**
- One full call lifecycle: initiated → answered → disposition logged

---

### 3) Queue Scoring with AI
**Why it matters:** Not all queue items are equal; scoring improves conversion efficiency by prioritizing the best next actions.

**Show live:**
- Queue ranking based on account/contact/context signals
- Priority changes when campaign rules or new signals are applied
- Agent queue focusing on highest-probability opportunities first

**Proof signal:**
- Show the same queue before/after scoring and explain top-ranked rationale

---

### 4) Compliance & Suppression Intelligence
**Why it matters:** Scale without violating policy or damaging brand.

**Show live:**
- Email/phone suppression checks
- Compliance-aware send/call gating
- Audit-ready traceability for why action was blocked/allowed

**Proof signal:**
- Show one suppressed target skipped automatically

---

### 5) QA + Revenue Intelligence Loop
**Why it matters:** Not just activity; quality and conversion performance are continuously improved.

**Show live:**
- Call QA scoring/disposition quality checks
- Pipeline/deal intelligence fields and activity trail
- Feedback loop to optimize messaging and sequencing

**Proof signal:**
- Before/after quality insight from real campaign sample

---

### 6) Organization-Exclusive AI Studio
**Why it matters:** Enterprises need AI outputs grounded in their own organization context, not generic templates.

**Show live:**
- Landing/email asset generation and refinement using org-specific context
- Approval-aware publishing flow
- Reuse in campaign execution with organization-specific voice

**Proof signal:**
- Generate one campaign asset, refine it with org context, and show publish-ready output

---

## Recommended Landing Page Order
1. Featured Intelligence Systems (6 cards)
2. Enterprise Readiness
3. Mid-page CTA
4. Detailed capabilities (existing)

This keeps discovery logical for enterprise buyers: **what's unique → why it's safe → how to engage**.

## 10-Minute Demo Flow (for Sales / Founder)
1. **Context Build (2 min):** problem intelligence + account context
2. **Execution (2 min):** AI voice + email orchestration
3. **Prioritization (2 min):** queue scoring with AI
4. **Governance (2 min):** suppression/compliance + controls
5. **Optimization (1 min):** QA + revenue intelligence loop
6. **Org AI Close (1 min):** organization-exclusive AI content output
