# Organization Intelligence in Campaign Creation — User Guide\n\n## Quick Start\n\n### What is Organization Intelligence?\nOrganization Intelligence is your organization's profile containing details about:\n- **Industries you serve**\n- **Customer personas and job titles**\n- **Geographic regions**\n- **Product/service offerings**\n- **Value propositions and positioning**\n\n### How to Use It in Campaign Creation\n\n1. **Start Creating a Campaign**\n   - Go to Client Portal → Campaigns → Create Campaign\n   - Fill in Channel and Basics steps\n\n2. **Navigate to Details Step**\n   - You'll see a blue \"Organization Intelligence\" card\n   - This only appears if you have an organization profile set up\n\n3. **Click \"Populate\" Button**\n   - The form will automatically fill in relevant campaign details\n   - Takes 1-2 seconds\n   - Shows \"Loading...\" while processing\n\n4. **Review & Adjust**\n   - All populated fields are pre-filled\n   - You can edit or add to them\n   - Existing values are preserved (no overwrites)\n\n### What Gets Populated?\n\n| Campaign Field | Source | Example |\n|---|---|---|\n| **Target Industries** | Your ICP & Industry | SaaS, Enterprise Software |\n| **Target Job Titles** | Your ICP Personas | CTO, VP Engineering, Director of IT |\n| **Target Regions** | Your Organization Geography | North America, Western Europe |\n| **Target Audience** | Company Size + Description | Mid-market SaaS companies (500-2000 employees) |\n| **Success Criteria** | Your Use Cases | Successfully execute cloud migration, digital transformation |\n\n### Smart Features\n\n- ✅ **No Duplicates**: Won't add industries/titles you already have\n- ✅ **Non-Destructive**: Won't overwrite your manual entries\n- ✅ **Safe to Use Multiple Times**: Can click \"Populate\" several times\n- ✅ **Preserves Edits**: Your custom changes are saved\n\n### Troubleshooting\n\n**\"No Organization Intelligence\" Error**\n- **Cause**: You haven't set up your organization profile yet\n- **Solution**: \n  1. Go to Client Portal → Settings → Organization Intelligence\n  2. Create your organization profile\n  3. Return to campaign creation\n\n**Fields Not Populating**\n- **Cause**: Those fields aren't configured in your organization profile\n- **Solution**: \n  1. Update your organization profile with more details\n  2. Try again\n\n**Want to Populate Selected Fields Only?**\n- Currently, clicking \"Populate\" adds all available data\n- You can then delete any fields you don't want\n- Contact support for selective population feature requests\n\n## Best Practices\n\n1. **Keep Organization Intelligence Updated**\n   - Review quarterly\n   - Update when business changes\n   - Add detailed personas and use cases\n\n2. **Customize After Populating**\n   - Populate gives you a great starting point\n   - Add campaign-specific details\n   - Remove irrelevant suggestions\n\n3. **Use as a Template**\n   - Save time by populating instead of typing\n   - Use for multiple similar campaigns\n   - Maintain consistency across campaigns\n\n## FAQ\n\n**Q: Will \"Populate\" delete my existing campaign data?**\nA: No. It only adds missing fields and appends to list fields (industries, titles, regions).\n\n**Q: Can I undo the populate action?**\nA: You can manually remove any unwanted entries, or refresh the page (if you haven't saved yet).\n\n**Q: Do I have to use Organization Intelligence?**\nA: No, all campaign details are optional. Fill in what you want, skip the rest.\n\n**Q: How often is Organization Intelligence updated?**\nA: It stays in sync with your organization profile. Updates immediately when you modify your profile.\n\n**Q: Can I populate different organizations?**\nA: The populate feature uses your primary organization. For multiple organizations, you'll need to populate separately for each.\n\n## Related Resources\n\n- 📖 [Organization Intelligence Setup Guide](../CLIENT_PORTAL_INTELLIGENCE.md)\n- 🎥 [Campaign Creation Tutorial](#)\n- 💬 [Support & Questions](#)\n"
